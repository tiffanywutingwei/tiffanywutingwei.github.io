Learning with Google Translate

Design for value-added product experience for users around the world

Project Type

Summer internship as a UX designer with Google Translate

Role

I designed and prepared to launch a new feature on Translate Android app a cross-functional product team, consisting of engineers, PMs and researchers

Duration

Summer 2018 (14 weeks)

Skills

Rapid prototyping, redlining, storytelling, cross-functional collaboration, design project management

Introduction

Being a UX designer in industry

This summers, I worked as a UX design intern on Google Translate team to launch a new feature for the better language learning experience with our product.

*** This project is subject a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) ***

Process

Iterate with rapid product development cycles

In 10 weeks, I collaborated with 2 engineer interns and full-time employees supervised the team to research, design, prototype, test and eventually prepare to launch the new feature on Google Translate app.

A learning experience for users and me as a designer
